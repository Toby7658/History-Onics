About Us



Welcome to HistoryOnics, your source for getting to know more about Ireland. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of information, with a focus on touristic knowledge and hidden gems all roled up in a history lesson.Founded in 2018, HistoryOnics has come a long way from its beginnings in Co. Clare. When we first started out, our passion for historical knowledge of Ireland drove us to do tons of research, so that Historyonics can offer you the world's most advanced History site. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website. We hope you enjoy our guides to Ireland as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
